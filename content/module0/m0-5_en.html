Important notice

Take your time. Don’t rush. The training doesn’t need to be done all at once. These courses have been carefully designed for you to gradually build your personal and professional knowledge.

Self-care

Some subjects covered in this training may cause an emotional reaction. Be kind to yourself. If you require support as you work through the material, please reach out to someone, such as:

  • a colleague
  • your manager
  • a friend
  • a Friendship Centre
  • your departmental Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
  • a community Elder
  • Health Canada's 24-hour crisis line
