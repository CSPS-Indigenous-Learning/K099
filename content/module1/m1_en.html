Cultural self-reflection: what I know and what I don’t know

Can a person have biases without even knowing? The answer is YES! In this course, we want to demystify the idea of bias and unconscious biases and help you gain the competencies to build respectful relationships with First Nations, Métis and Inuit.

To develop these competencies, you need to start by looking at yourself. What do you know and what don’t you know about culture?

This self-reflection is essential to understanding what you think and believe now. To be able to openly address any misunderstandings or biases you may have developed, you need first to be aware of them.
