Question of

True or False ?

Most people are aware of their own prejudices.

Answer: False

Research suggests that bias occurs in most people, even in those who are convinced they are unbiased. We may be aware of some of our biases. Most of us are not aware of our “unconscious” or hidden biases. These form part of what is normal to us, so we don’t realize that we hold them.

Question of

True or False ?

Discrimination refers to your thoughts, while stereotyping refers to how you behave.

Answer: False

Stereotyping is how we think.

Prejudice is how we feel.

Discrimination is how we behave.

Question of

True or False ?

Creating culturally safe relationships with Indigenous people requires understanding the significance and impact of race on our perceptions, beliefs and actions.

Answer: True

A key part of Indigenous cultural safety is awareness of our beliefs, attitudes and behaviours. As well, it requires knowledge about the impact of race on the lives of people who experience racism. It involves critical assessment of our ideas about race and racism.
