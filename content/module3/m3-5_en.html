Cultural safety

As a public service employee, you are expected to respect the Values and Ethics Code for the Public Sector:

  • Respect for democracy
  • Respect for people
  • Integrity
  • Stewardship
  • Excellence

Cultural safety supports these values. It means that our decisions, actions, processes, policies and systems include the needs and rights of all people. It means that we do not discriminate.

There is power imbalance between Indigenous peoples and non-Indigenous peoples in Canada. To create a culturally safe environment for Indigenous peoples, we need to examine the historical and political factors that created a power imbalance between Indigenous peoples and non-Indigenous peoples in Canada.

Definition : Cultural safety

Cultural safety focuses on responsive services. Its goal is to create equitable health and socio-economic outcomes for Indigenous people.
