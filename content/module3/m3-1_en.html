Cultural awareness

As a public service employee, Cultural awareness is an important part of your daily job. It means being aware of the impacts of identity, history and social factors. When you are more aware, you develop greater respect. This will help you better serve the public, which in Canada includes First Nations, Métis and Inuit.

Definition : Cultural awareness

Cultural awareness is understanding the cultural differences between groups, while not assigning a value to those differences.
