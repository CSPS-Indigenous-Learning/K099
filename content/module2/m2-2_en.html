Your worldview

Many of the characteristics featured in the cultural iceberg are about underlying assumptions and beliefs. These assumptions shape the way we see and interpret the world around us. They represent our world view.

Self-reflection: What did you learn about your culture, assumptions and beliefs? What do the characteristics of your culture tell you about your world view?

Look at your answers

Look at your answers. There are no right answers to this exercise. The objective is to realize that there are many layers to culture and a deeper understanding is often needed.

Even from this short list you can see that culture is multi-layered and complex. The characteristics cover many different Indigenous groups. You would likely find that each group would construct their “cultural iceberg” in a different way.
